About Ella

Ella is a London-based HCPC registered Health Psychologist and Chartered member of the British Psychological Society (BPS).

She works as a Senior Research Fellow at the Centre for Appearance Research (CAR), a world-leading research centre based at the University of the West of England (UWE) in Bristol.

Experience with Psychosocial Support

Ella has experience of working and volunteering with large organisations, charities, the NHS both in the UK and overseas. Ella has also worked with patients with chronic pain, insomnia, and cancer, to help them live well with and manage he psychosocial aspects of these conditions. Ella runs bespoke psychoeducational workshops on stress and managing competing demands both in-person and online.

Stress Management and Competing Demands

She conducts evidence-based psychoeducational workshops on managing stress and competing demands, both online and face-to-face. These interactive sessions provide psychoeducational information about the wider impact of stress and burnout, along with evidence-backed strategies to combat stress and effectively prioritize competing demands. These sessions are well-suited to groups and can be tailored to the needs of your organization.
